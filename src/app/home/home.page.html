Euroleague

The EuroLeague, known as the Turkish Airlines EuroLeague for sponsorship reasons, is a European professional basketball club competition.

The league is widely recognised as the top-tier league in Europe. The league consists of 18 teams

(though the three Russian teams are currently suspended because of the 2022 Russian invasion of Ukraine),

of which 16 are given long-term licences and wild cards,[2] making the league a semi-closed league.[3][4]

The league has been organized by Euroleague Basketball since its formation in 2000.
